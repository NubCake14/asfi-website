InSanity

Maintain your sanity through means of your own choosing. Shall you consume a mysterious alcoholic substance, or will you take the lives of others to enrich your own? Fight your own demons, or lose it all.

Gameplay

Enjoy the luxury of mechanics from your favorite platformers, along with a 2D click-and-shoot system and a fully manageable inventory. Expand your arsenal, and make life-dependent decisions on what is needed, and what is better off left behind.
